Our Mission

Built for
Athletes

Paradise Sports Performance is now recognized as the official destination for all individual athletes and sports teams in the tri-county. We have put together the most complete system of its kind. Paradise Sports is a top notch and professional facility with experienced and knowledgeable staff, cutting edge science and technology, guidance in recovery and nutrition, and sport specific development.

Our goal as a staff is to not only improve athletes physically but also emotionally and functioning. We want our athletes to be leaders in the facility as well as in their respective schools and communities.

We have dedicated Paradise Sports Performance to offering the very best in athletic sports performance training. There's a reason why more athletes and teams train here than any other private facility in Cuyahoga Falls.
